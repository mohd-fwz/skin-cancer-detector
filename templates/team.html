About Our Project

We are a team of passionate computer science engineering students dedicated to leveraging artificial intelligence for healthcare innovation. Our project, DermaDetectAI, combines machine learning expertise with a commitment to making early skin cancer detection more accessible.

Using the HAM10000 dataset and deep learning techniques, we've developed a tool that can assist in the preliminary identification of skin lesions, contributing to the important cause of early cancer detection.
